This folder is where your images go.

LOGO:
  Place your Cognera logo here as:  cognera-logo.png
  It will automatically appear in the header, article pages, and the logo transition animation.

ARTICLE IMAGES:
  You can also place article cover images here, then reference them in data/articles.ts as:
  image: "/images/your-filename.jpg"

AUTHOR PHOTOS:
  Same — place them here and reference as:
  author: { image: "/images/author-photo.jpg" }
